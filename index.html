Welcome to our lab, Dr. Ferris, our main curious head, leads the way! We’ve got a dynamic mix of postdocs, grad students, and undergrads from all corners of the globe,all united by our shared quest to answer the intriguing questions that pop into our heads.We spend most of our time working hard, but there's always room for a little chit-chat and, of course, plenty of fun. Our weekly meetings? They’re like life update sessions, where we all share what’s new and exciting. The highlight, though, is MAD Science—everyone’s favorite part—where Dr. Ferris dazzles us with his latest mind-blowing, out-of-this-world inventions. It’s a blend of serious science and a touch of magic that keeps us inspired and motivated.
